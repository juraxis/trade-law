# Ruling Digest Template

## Structure

Generate CROSS ruling research reports with this structure. Follow citation conventions from `reference/formatting-standards.md`.

---

### Header Block

```
CROSS RULING RESEARCH DIGEST — DRAFT WORK PRODUCT

Date:           {current date}
Subject:        {product type or classification issue researched}
Headings:       {HTS headings covered}
Prepared by:    AI Classification Research Assistant
Status:         DRAFT — For Attorney/Broker Review
```

---

### 1. Research Scope

- **Product/issue researched:** {description of what was searched}
- **Search terms used:** {list the actual web_search queries executed}
- **Date range of rulings found:** {earliest} to {most recent}
- **Total rulings reviewed:** {number}
- **Relevant rulings selected:** {number}

---

### 2. Ruling Summary Table

| # | Ruling | Date | Product | Classification | GRI | Similarity | Evidence Quality | Status |
|---|--------|------|---------|----------------|-----|------------|-----------------|--------|
| 1 | {NY/HQ Number} | {date} | {brief description} | {HTS subheading} | {GRI #} | {H/M/L} | {Verified/Identified/Unverifiable} | {Active/Revoked} |
| 2 | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |

---

### 3. Individual Ruling Analyses

For each ruling selected:

#### Ruling {#}: CBP Ruling {NY/HQ} {Number} (dated {Date})

**Product Classified:** {full description of the product in the ruling}

**Classification:** {HTS subheading}, HTSUS

**GRI Applied:** GRI {number} — {brief explanation of application}

**Key Reasoning:**
{2-4 sentence summary of the legal analysis. Quote the dispositive language if available.}

**Section/Chapter Notes Cited:** {list any notes relied upon}

**Explanatory Notes Cited:** {list any WCO ENs referenced}

**Factual Similarity to Subject Product:**
- **Rating:** High / Medium / Low
- **Matching factors:** {what is similar}
- **Distinguishing factors:** {what is different}

**Evidence Quality:** {Verified / Identified / Unverifiable} — {basis: e.g., "full text retrieved via cached snippet" or "ruling number and HTS visible, reasoning not retrievable"}

**Status:** Active / Revoked by {ruling} / Modified by {ruling}

---

### 4. Analysis

#### A. Consensus Position
{What classification do the majority of rulings support? How strong is the consensus?}

#### B. Evolution of CBP Practice
{Has CBP's approach to classifying this product type changed over time? Are recent rulings consistent with older ones?}

#### C. Conflicts and Outliers
{Any rulings that reach a different classification? What factual distinctions explain the difference? Are there unresolved conflicts?}

#### D. Authority Assessment
{Relative weight of the rulings found: HQ vs. NY, recent vs. old, factually close vs. distant}

#### E. Gaps in Ruling Coverage
{What aspects of the subject product are not addressed by existing rulings? What questions remain open?}

---

### 5. Judicial Review Status

{Have any of the cited rulings or their classification positions been addressed by CIT or CAFC?}
- If yes: cite the decision and note whether the ruling was upheld or overturned
- If no: note the absence of judicial review

---

### 6. Recommendations

- How the rulings inform the classification analysis
- Whether additional rulings should be searched (broader or narrower queries)
- Whether a binding ruling request to CBP is advisable
- Any flags triggered from `reference/human-review-triggers.md`

---

### 7. Disclaimer

Include the **Condensed Disclaimer** from `reference/disclaimers.md`.
